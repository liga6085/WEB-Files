Lily Gabriel

My name is Lily Gabriel and I am a design student with a background in robotics and art. After working in robotics for seven years, I decided to pivot and study Creative Technology and Design at the University of Colorado Boulder. This allowed me to integrate my artwork into more technical pieces and work towards more people-oriented work.

My portfolio consists of a variety of mediums, from artwork to historical studies to robotics. All of my work is open source and I document my process to the best of my ability. You can find examples below or a more extensive catalogue on my "Portfolio" page. You can find all my contact information and social media sites on my "Contact Me" page.
