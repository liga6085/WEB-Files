The Dark Crystal

"The Dark Crystal" is a Henson product released in 1982.

Plot

The story follows a young gelfling, named Jen, and his journey to repair the Crystal of Truth.

The movie opens with a flashback, recalling the shattering of the Crystal of Truth during a skirmish between the Skeksis and urRu. 1000 years later we find Jen playing music by a river, he then receives new that his parental figure, UrSu, is dying.
